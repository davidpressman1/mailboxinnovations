Common questions

Short answers now, more detail when we talk.

Can I keep using my current Gmail account?

In most cases, you will sign into a new business account that looks almost the same as Gmail, but under your company domain. We can also set up forwarding from your old personal address if needed.

Do you provide the Microsoft or Google licenses?

We help you choose the right plan and set everything up. You can pay Microsoft or Google directly or work with a reseller. We do not lock you into a contract for the licenses.

Will there be downtime?

We plan changes so that you can keep reading mail while DNS updates spread. There can be a short window where messages flow to both old and new mailboxes, and we explain how to handle that.

Can you manage DNS and email security long term?

Yes. If you want us to keep an eye on your DNS, SPF, DKIM, and DMARC records, we can include that as part of ongoing management.
